Welcome to GreenU. We are a community of change-makers dedicated to environmental conservation and sustainable living. Through tree planting programs, innovative waste management, and community education, we strive to reduce the ecological footprint and create a healthier planet for future generations. Together with you, we realize a sustainable Earth.

Our Vision

"To become the leading catalyst (platform) in Indonesia for realizing a sustainable ecosystem and a society with high ecological awareness for a healthier Earth."

Our Mission

To achieve this Vision, GreenU will focus on four mission pillars berikut:

  • Conservation and Nature Restoration: Taking concrete action for conservation, including mass tree planting programs, critical land restoration, and biodiversity protection.
  • Community Education and Empowerment: Providing educational platforms, workshops, and practical training on eco-friendly lifestyles, waste management, and renewable energy.
  • Innovative Waste Solutions: Developing and implementing innovative solutions in waste management, ranging from promoting the Zero Waste Lifestyle and Community Recycling Banks (Bank Sampah), to encouraging creative recycling practices (Upcycling).
  • Advocacy and Public Engagement: Encouraging collaboration with various parties (government, private sector, and individuals) to support pro-environmental policies and increase public participation in sustainability issues.
